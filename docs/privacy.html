Privacy Policy

Health+  ·  Effective date: June 19, 2025

Health+ ("the app", "we", "us") is an iOS application that aggregates your personal health and fitness data in one place. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Data We Access

The app may access the following categories of data, depending on the permissions you grant:

  • Apple Health (HealthKit) – steps, workouts, heart rate, sleep, and other health metrics you choose to share.
  • WHOOP – recovery score, HRV, strain, and sleep data via the WHOOP OAuth API, only if you connect your WHOOP account.
  • Apple Sign In – your name and email address if you choose to create an account.
  • Habits & Workouts – custom habits, workout logs, and program data you enter directly in the app.

2. How Your Data Is Used

  • All health data is processed on your device only and is never uploaded to our servers.
  • Health data is used solely to display dashboards, track progress, and power the app's features.
  • If you use the AI Habits setup chat, your typed messages are sent to Anthropic's API using the API key you supply. No health data is sent to Anthropic.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising or analytics purposes.

3. Data Storage

  • Habits, workout logs, and settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults and Core Data frameworks.
  • WHOOP OAuth tokens are stored in the iOS Keychain on your device.
  • No personal or health data is transmitted to or stored on servers we operate.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Apple HealthKit – governed by Apple's privacy policy.
  • WHOOP API – governed by WHOOP's privacy policy. We only request read-only access to your WHOOP data.
  • Anthropic Claude API – used only for the Habits AI setup chat if you provide your own API key. Your health data is never sent to this API. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Sign in with Apple – governed by Apple's privacy policy.

5. Children's Privacy

Health+ is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

6. Your Rights

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time by deleting the app from your device. You may also revoke HealthKit permissions at any time in the iOS Settings app under Privacy & Security → Health.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the effective date at the top of this page when changes are made. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
